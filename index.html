🌍 About SkillBridge Africa

Our Mission

🎯

Purpose

Create self-sustaining livelihoods through skills training.

🌍

Vision

A prosperous Africa where every young person has a trade.

🤝

Values

Integrity, Excellence, Inclusivity, and Innovation.

Our Story

SkillBridge Africa (SBA) was founded to address the skills gap in Africa. We believe that vocational training is the key to unlocking economic growth. SBA is a business, not a school – we provide practical, hands-on training that prepares students for real-world employment or entrepreneurship. With over 500 courses, an AI Tutor, and blockchain certificates, we are building the future of African vocational education.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enroll in a course? ▼
Click "Browse All Courses" → select any course → click "Enroll" (or "Buy Now" for paid courses). You must be logged in.
What is the difference between $10 Full Access and $3.50 Read‑Only? ▼
$10 Full Access includes all modules, AI Tutor support, and a Blockchain Certificate. $3.50 Read‑Only lets you view all modules but you cannot get a certificate or use the AI Tutor.
How do I get my certificate? ▼
Complete all modules in a course, then click "Mark Complete". A unique certificate code will be generated. Go to the "Verify" tab to check its authenticity.
What payment methods are accepted? ▼
We support EcoCash, M‑Pesa, Visa, Mastercard, Bank Transfer, Orange Money, Airtel Money, MTN Mobile, and MoMo.
