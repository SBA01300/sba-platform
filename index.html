❓ Frequently Asked Questions

🏫 Is Rise Africa Skills (RAS) a school? ▼
No – RAS is NOT a school.

Rise Africa Skills (RAS) is a skills development company that provides practical, hands-on vocational training. We don't issue formal academic degrees – we give you real-world skills you can use immediately.

💰 Why is a full course only $10? ▼

Because we believe skills should be affordable.

Your $10 gets you: 8+ modules, PDFs, AI Tutor, Blockchain Certificate, and more!

🏪 Can I start a business after taking a course? ▼

YES – that's exactly what RAS is designed for!

Grace Mwangi took our Soap Making course, started her own business, and now employs 3 people. You can be next.

🤷 What if I don't have $10? ▼

We've got you covered.

  • 🎁 Free starter course – "Dishwashing Liquid" is completely free!
  • 👀 Read-Only access – For only $3.50
  • 📚 Bundle deals – Get 5 courses for $40
🔗 Is the Blockchain Certificate real? ▼

YES – it's 100% real and verifiable.

Anyone can verify your certificate on our website. Employers can check your skills instantly.

⏱️ How long does it take to complete a course? ▼

It's self-paced – YOU decide.

  • ⚡ Finish in 2 days – Full-time study
  • 📅 Finish in 2 weeks – 1 hour per day
  • 🗓️ Finish in 2 months – At your own pace
📱 How do I access my course after buying? ▼

It's easy – you can access from anywhere.

  • 💻 On computer – Open the website
  • 📱 On phone – Mobile-friendly
  • 📥 Offline access – Download PDFs and study offline
